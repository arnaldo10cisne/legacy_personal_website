This simple website was made to test the idea of Responsive Design, and, to be more specific, the idea of a "Mobile First" Layout, that is, designing a website with the idea that it is going to look great on mobile devices first, and the end product will be escalated to fit a full desktop screen. "Metroidvanias" are just a genre of videogames focused on exploration.
